About Harvest City Church


Welcome to Harvest City Church!

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about our church. You’ll discover that we are more than just a weekend service. We are a family that connects, grows, and builds together. From the moment you walk in, no matter your age, background, or life story, you’ll feel like you’re home. Our passion is to provide a place where people just like you can have a life-changing encounter with God, develop meaningful relationships, discover your purpose, and make an eternal impact in the world around you.
